Delicious Steak Penne Pasta

Ingredients

alfredo sauce

  • 1 stick of softened butter
  • 300 ml of heavy whipping cream
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp. Tony's Creole Seasong, recommended. Add more or less to taste
  • 1 cup parmesean cheese

Steak and Pasta

    <
  • 1 tbsp. olive oil
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 3/4 box of uncooked penne pasta
  • 1 pound sirloin steak, cut into strips
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. In a medium sized pot, bring 2 1/2 cups of water to a boil, add a heavy pinch of salt

Steak and Pasta

  1. While waiting for the water to boil, cut steak into thin strips, add 1 tbsp of oil into a medium sized frying pan on medium-high heat, and add steak, salt and pepper, cook Steak for about 2 minutes on each side.
  2. Once water has reached a rolling boil, add uncooked pasta. Cook for about 11 minutes
  3. Take cooked steak out of the pan and set aside for later

Alfredo sauce

  1. In the same medium sized pan used for the steak, add the butter. Let melt heat should be set to medium high
  2. Once the butter has melted, add heavy whipping cream, stir to combine
  3. Add seasonings, and stir again to incorporate
  4. Bring cream, butter and seasonings to a boil, and reduce to a simmer. Repeat until the sauce has thickened to your liking
  5. Add steak that was set aside earlier, back into the alfredo sauce, Mix.
  6. Add cooked pasta into the steak and alfredo sauce. Stir to combine
  7. Add the 1 cup of parmesean cheese to the steak alfredo pasta, and mix until cheese is completely melted
  8. Let cool for 2 minutes, and serve. Recipe feeds 4-6 people
