< favorite childhood book

The Cat in the Hat

Dr. Seuss

The story starts out with two children stuck inside on a cold rainy day. the kids sit and wait, and think upon their boredum, when all of the sudden, comes in Cat in the Hat! He greets them with questions about why they are sitting and doing nothing, he proposes that they make the best out of their day.

The kids fish became suspicious of this cats behavior, and told the kids not to engage with the cat in the hat. The cat proposes a game idea that involves said fish and the fish does not approve, so cat in the hat proceeds to try and impress the fish with all of his amazing tricks. However, the fish is still very unimpressed as the cat makes an absolute disaster of the house.

After the mess has been made and the kids and the fish are dismayed, the cat brings in a little red box, that he opens and two "things" pop out. The boy spots his mother out the window, and everyone begins to panic, except for the cat in the Hat he leaves momentarily and comes back in the house with a machine that begins cleansing the big mess that he made. The fish and the two kids sit there in excitement as they watch the Cat in the Hat work away at the house. The cat in the hat manages to make the house spotless just in time for the mother to come home and for the cat to get away without the mother sighting him.

The story ends with the mother coming back and the children and fish are exactly where they were sitting when the story began. The mother asks what the children did when she was away, and none of them can give her an answer to her question.
